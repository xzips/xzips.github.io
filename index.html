About

About Me

As a third-year Engineering Science student specializing in Aerospace Engineering at the University of Toronto, I am passionate about mathematics, science, aviation, and space exploration, and I am committed to applying my skills to practical engineering projects.

I have designed and developed a fully custom fixed-wing UAV, integrating skills in aerodynamics, control systems, and electronics. As the rocket avionics system lead in the University of Toronto Aerospace Team (UTAT), I continue to advance my expertise in CAD, PCB design, and simulation as part of the avionics subsystem.

Alongside my technical pursuits, I maintain a balanced lifestyle, regularly working out at the gym and practicing photography during my free time.
